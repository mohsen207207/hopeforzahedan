Hope for Zahedan

Help us build a 10-classroom school for underprivileged children in Zahedan, Iran

About the Project

Zahedan is one of the most underserved regions in Iran, where many children lack access to proper education. We aim to build a modern 10-classroom school to bring hope, knowledge, and a brighter future to these children. Your donation can help change lives and create lasting impact.

With a goal to raise $100,000, this project will cover construction, materials, and facilities for the school. Join us in making a difference!
